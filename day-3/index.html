Day 3: 13 February 2017

Posted by Anivarth Peesapati on Mon 13 February 2017 in Current Affairs

2 soldiers and 4 militants die in a new fire exchange

  • Of the 4 militants encountered, 2 of them belong to Hizbul Mujahideen and 2 others belong to Lashker-e-Taiba(L-eT)

  • Encounter took place in Kulgam, 70 KM to the south of Srinagar.

Nobel Burglary case cracked

  • Three days afer burglary in Nobel peace Laureate Kailash Satyarthi's apartment, Delhi police had cracked the case.

Plan to appoint single-point military advisor

  • Prime Minister Office is planning to appoint a single-point military advisor to obtain a better coordination between the armed forces.

  • The plan is to put a better framework first before the officer is appointed.

Chennai Oil spill reaches Mamallapuram

  • Chennai oil spill had reached the UNESCO world heritage site Mamallapuram(Mahabalipuram).

  • On January 28, 2017, two ships collided at the Kamarajar port causing a massive oil spill in the sea of the coast of Chennai.

Sathanur dam opened

  • Sathanur dam in Tiruvannamalai district of Tamil Nadu was opened by the district collector Prashant M Wadnere.

Centre to invest in Tech. Startups

  • Centre is planning to invest Rs. 2200 crores by 2019 in startups working on new technologies.

  • The investment will be made under the Electronic Development Fund(EDF).

R. Ashwin creates a new record

  • Of the 45 test matches played by Ravichandran Ashwin, the 30 year old had bagged the fastest 250 wickets record.

India wins the T20 World's cup for blind

  • India defeated Pakistan in the final of the T20 world cup for Blind.

  • Match was held at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium in Bangalore.

India wins badminton tournment

  • Indian duo comprising of Arjun Mandatil Ravichandran and Ravichandran Shlok had won the Iran Fajr badminton tournament held at Tehran on February 12, 2017.

World Women's chess championship

  • Padmini Rout had made into the ' Round of 32 ' after a draw with Armenia's Elina Danielian at the World Women's chess championship held at Tehran.

  • North Korea has t est fired a ballistic missile which landed into the waters of Japan.

  • Japan's prime minister Shinzo Abe had condemned the test.

Steinmeier is the new German President

  • Frank Walter Steinmeir was elected as the new president of Germany .

  • He was the former foreign minister for Germany.

  • He will assume office from 19 March 2017.

Taniguchi Dies at 69

  • Famours Manga legend and cartoon artist Taniguchi died in Tokyo at the age of 69.
