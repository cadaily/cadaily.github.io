Day 6: 16 February 2017

Posted by Anivarth Peesapati on Thu 16 February 2017 in Current Affairs

ISRO launches 104 satellites at a time

  • ISRO launches 104 satellites at a time with PSLV(Polar Satellite launch vehicle) C-37.

  • Most of the satellites belonged to 6 different nations.

  • With the successful flight PSLV has created a record with 38 successful missions out of 39 missions.

Cloud seeding over Maharastra

  • Maharashtra Government in coordination with Indian institute of Tropical Meteorology(IITM) will do cloud seeding to create rain over the drought affected areas.

Nod for Sabarimala Airport

  • Kerala government has given approval for the Sabarimala airport. It will be a Green Field airport.

  • The move is to cut the traffic on the road.

Over 68% polling registered in Uttarakand polling

  • Votes were cast for 628 candidates for 13 districts in the hilly state of Uttarakand.

  • Polling percentage of 68% is till 5PM and the number could even go as high as 70%.

UP phase-2 elections record 65% polling

  • Uttar Pradesh Phase – 2 elections has seen a 65% turnout of the electorate. The data is till 5PM.

  • 67 seats of 11 districts went for polls in phase – 2.

Rawat warns locals not to interfere in operations

  • Army chief General Bipin Rawat has warned civilians not to interfere in regular army operations.

  • Recently due to the interference of locals in weeding out some militants, militants have used this as opportunity and fled away.

In-principle approval for merger of SBI with 5 of its subsidiaries

  • Cabinet has given in-principle approval to merge SBI with State Bank of Bikener and Jaipur, State Bank of Hyderabad, State Bank of Patiala, State Bank of Mysore, State Bank of Travancore.

  • This merger is expected to improve operational efficiency and save Rs. 10,000.

Constitution Bench to hear over Delhi – LG spat

  • A constitution bench will hear the Delhi government appeals on whether the Lieutenant Governor has the unilateral power to administer Delhi.

  • An earlier bench on the same case has given a verdict that Lieutenant governor has the authority over police, land, law and order and services.

Bajaj Vehicles BS – IV complaint

  • Bajaj Auto has said that all its vehicles would comply to BS – IV norms from April 1, 2017.

  • Environmental Pollution Control Authority(EPCA) has set the deadline till April 1, 2017, after which sales of vehicles which don't comply to BS – IV is prohibited.

HAL to issue IPO

  • Hindustan Aeronautics limited(HAL) is planning to divest 10% of its stake via equity and will soon submit documents to stock exchange regulator SEBI.

Rohan Bopanna enters quarterfinals

  • Rohan Bopanna who is on ATP world tour event along with Thomas Berdych in Rotterdam has entered the quarter finals.

Luftansa to name one of its aircrafts “Delhi”

  • Luftansa, the German Airline is planning to name one of its Airbus A380 aircraft “Delhi”

  • Luftansa is planning to increase its market share in India.

Trump criticizes Intelligence agencies

  • American President Donald Trump has criticized the Intelligence agencies NSA and FBI over leaking sensitive information to media.

Referendum over new constitution

  • Sri Lankan Government is planning to introduce a referendum for its new constitution.

  • Sri Lankan President Maitripala Sirisena had promised a new constitution in his election campaign.
