Day 4: 14 February 2017

Posted by Anivarth Peesapati on Tue 14 February 2017 in Current Affairs

Reliance to service US Navy Ships

  • Anil Ambani led Reliance defence Engineering Ltd. had partnered with US Navy to service and maintain the vessels of its seventh fleet consisting of 100 vessels.

  • Reliance defence shipyard is located at Pipanav in Gujarat.

Karnataka amends PCA act

  • Karnataka government has made its amendment to the Protection of Cruelty to Animals act 1960 to allow kambla and bullock cart racing.

  • Governer is yet to refer the bill to the president of India.

Verdict on Vypak Scam

  • Supreme court comprising of Chief Justice J S Khehar and justices Kurian joseph and Arun Mishra have given a verdict on Vypam scam to cancel the admissions given to 634 students.

Centre is planning to ask states to set the factories threshold limit

  • Central government is planning to push the burden of overhauling the F actories Act of 1948 on the state governments to set the threshold limit.

  • At present, the Factories Act of 1948 recognizes an establishment with 10 or more workers and using electricity or 20 or more workers and not using electricity as a factory.

L&T, european firm to make missiles

  • L&T has partnered with a european firm MBDA missile systems for making missile in India.

  • L&T will hold 51% stake in the Joint Venture and remaining 49% stake will be owned by MBDA missile systems.

Zimbabwe looses to India

  • Zimbabwe which made 60 runs in 28.5 overs lost to I ndia in ICC women's world cup qualifier in Colombo, Sri Lanka.

Bangladesh looses to India

  • India has won the Paytm one – off cricket test match.

  • Bangladesh lost by 208 runs.

Islamabad HC bans Valentine's day events

  • In a ruling, Islamabad High Court had banned the Valentine's day events stating them as unIslamic.

Tensions escalate on North Korea's missile test

  • China had asked all the other countries to focus on other factors over North Korea's behaviour and not just bringing the pressure on Beijing. On February 12, 2017 North Korea had tested a ballistic missile which the North Korean government termed successful.

Saeed is planning to float a political party

  • UN – designated terrorist Hafiz Saeed is planning to start a political party for his group Jamma'at – ud – Dawa(JuD).

  • The main plan is to prevent the cadre from shifting to other Islamic groups like IS.

La La Land fails, but Dev Patel bad an award at Bafta

  • La La Land which was expected to get an award at Britain's Bafta failed to get an award. On the other hand Indian Hollywood actor Dev Patel secured one.

  • Dev Patel was awarded for his supporting character in the movie Lion.
