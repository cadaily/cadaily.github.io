Day 2: 12 February 2017

Posted by Anivarth Peesapati on Sun 12 February 2017 in Current Affairs

Up records 64% polling in First phase

  • Polling held for 73 constituencies in 15 districts of Uttar Pradesh.

  • 839 candidates contested the election. 77 of them were women.

  • 64% polling has been recorded in first phase. 2.59 crore citizens utilized their Right to Vote .

Solar Power on INS Sarvekshak

  • INS Sarvekshak has been installed with a 5.4KW solar power system for generating a share of power required on-board.

  • This will replace diesel alternator which has been generating 4.4KW power thus reducing the carbon footprint by 60 tonnes.

Vijay Joshi to replace MK Razdan as PTI editor-in-chief

  • Senior Journalist Vijay Jo shi has been appointed as the new Editor-in-chief by Press Trust of India (PTI).

  • His predecessor was MK Razdan.

Interceptor missile test successful

  • Developed indigenously by DRDO, interceptor missile was successfully tested on February 11, 2017.

  • This missile comes under the class of Indian Ballastic Missile Defence ( BMD ).

  • This missile can destroy threats from exo and endo atmospheres.

  • At present only 4 nations in the world have this capability.

Five names for the post of SC Judges

  • Union Law Ministry has given 5 names for the appointment of Supreme Court Judges.

  • They are:

    • Naveen Sinha (Chief Justice of the Rajastan High Court)

    • M Santhanagouder (Chief Justice of the Kerla High Court)

    • Dipak Gupta (Chief Justice of the Chattisgarh High Court)

    • S K Kaul (Chief Justice of the Madra s High Court)

    • Justice Abdul Nazeer (Karnataka High court)

LIC increases its shareholding in Infosys to 7.02%

  • Life Insurance Corporation(LIC) has increased its shareholdings in Infosys amidst the ongoing tensions within Infosys current board.

C K Birla Group Firm sells Ambassador

  • Hindustan Motors owned by C K Birla Group has inked a deal to sell Ambassador to European Auto major Peugeot for 80 crores.

ITC eyes on Health Care

  • ITC Ltd. Is planning to enter health care by setting up mutli-speciality hospitals.

Rcom and Idea post losses because of Jio

  • Reliance communications and I dea cellular have posted a net loss for the quarter ending December 2016 mainly due to the promotional offers being offered by Reliance Jio.

New batting coach for Kings XI Punjab

  • J Arun Kumar has been appointed as the batting coach for Kings XI Punjab.

New executive order on Immigration – USA

  • Donal Trump administration might consider a new executive order for the controversial immigration order banning people from seven Muslim-Majority states.

  • The move might be to win the legal battle.

China expels Korean Christian missionaries

  • China has expelled 32 missionaries from South Korea which have done their work in China for over a decade now.

  • It is being widely believed that this is China's retaliation against Seoul's plan to host Terminal High Altitude Area Defence(THAAD).
