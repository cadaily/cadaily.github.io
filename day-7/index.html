Day 7: 17 February 2017

Posted by Anivarth Peesapati on Fri 17 February 2017 in Current Affairs

Palaniswami is the 13th chief minister of TN

  • Edappadi K. Palaniswami was sworn in as the chief minister of Tamil Nadu by the Governor Ch. Vidyasagar Rao

  • Mr. Palaniswami said that he will take a floor test to prove his majority in the assembly.

Reliance and Dassault form a JV

  • French aerospace defence major Dassault has formed a Joint Venture with Reliance Aerostructures Limited for the supply of 36 Rafale fighter jets ordered by India.

  • The new JV is named Dassault Reliance Aerospace Limited(DRAL) with Anil Ambani holding 51% stake and the remaining 49% by Dassault.

Delhi Blasts accused acquitted

  • People who were accused for blasts which occurred on October 29, 2005 in busy places of Delhi killing 80 people and injuring over 200 people were acquitted of all charges.

Bihar Babus cannot consume liquor even outside the state

  • In an amendment to the new Bihar State Prohibition and Excise Act, 2016 , Bihar Babu's cannot consume liquor even outside the state.

  • Earlier they were prohibited to drink liquor when on duty.

KKNP nuclear power plant synchronized with the grid

  • Kundankulam Nuclear Power Project's( KKNP ) first reactor which was shut down on November 26 was synchronized with the grid.

Vikas Swarup appointed High commissioner to Canada

  • Ministry of External Affairs(MEA) has appointed prominent writer Vikas Swarup as the new High commissioner to Canada.

  • The move is to push better relations with Canada.

Tata Motors and Microsoft ink a deal

  • Tata Motors and Microsoft has inked a deal to make driving more fun and safer.

  • The move is to use AI(Artificial Intelligence), IoT(Internet of Things) and machine learning technologies to make it better for the car users.

Slunwei invests in Truebil

  • Chinese venture capital fund Shunwei Capital has made an investment of $3 million in the pre owned cars startup Truebil.

COA to review Deloitte Audit reports

  • Committee Of Administrators(COA) appointed by the Supreme Court to look into the Financial matters of BCCI will examine the Deloitte reports.

  • Deloitte was appointed by BCCI in 2015 as a part of its transformational project.

Talks on new constitution

  • Sri Lankan government is speeding up its process to introduce new constitution.

  • Sri Lankan president had earlier promised to introduce a new constitution in his election campaign.

Graphene from soya bean

  • Scientists from melbourne have discovered that high quality graphene can be manufactured from soybeans.
