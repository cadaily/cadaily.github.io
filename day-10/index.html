Day 10: 20 February 2017

Posted by Anivarth Peesapati on Mon 20 February 2017 in Current Affairs

61% polling recorded in 3rd phase in UP

  • 61% polling was recorded in the 3 rd phase polling in Uttar Pradesh .

  • There were 60 seats that went for polling in the 12 districts.

ICMR signs MOU with Monash

  • Indian Council for Medical Research(ICMR) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding(MOU) with Australian university Monash.

  • The research between these institutes will be focussed on combating dengue.

T. R. Zelian steps down as Nagaland CM

  • Nagaland Chief Minster T. R. Zelian has decided to step down because of the ongoing turmoil in the state.

Water scarcity in Bengaluru

  • Bangalore Water Supply and Sewerage Board(BWSSB) has hired around 25 private tankers in order to get out of the water scarcity crisis.

2 maoists killed

  • In an operation by Special Task Force, District Reserve Guard and Telangana;s Greyhounds, 2 maoists belonging to the banned CPI(Moist) were killed in two separate encounters in the Baster region in Chattisgarh.

Altmas Kabir no more

  • Former Chief Justice of India Altmas Kabir died at the age of 68.

  • He served as the 39th Chief Justice of India.

Idea, Vodafone likely to merfe this month

  • Aditya Birla group Idea cellular and Vodafone Idea are likely to merge by this month.

  • The merged entity will have the largest market share and is expected to provide an enhanced customer experience.

Havells buys a unit of Lloyd

  • Havells India Ltd. has agreed to buy a unit of Lloyd Electric and Engineering Ltd. for a $231 million.

Paytm to bring in Rs. 600 crores

  • Paytm is expected to invest Rs. 600 crores in the upcoming months to increase its market share of mobile wallet payments.

Smith is the new RPS captain

  • Steven smith , Australia's Test skipper will be the new Rising Pune Supergaints captain.

  • M. S. Dhoni was the former captain for this team.

Pollock to replace Shastri at ICC

  • Shaun Pollock has been appointed as the representative from the media on the ICC's cricket committee.

  • Ravi Shastri was the former representative for this post.

Failed peace talks between Isreal, Egypt and Jordan

  • Isreali Prime Miniter Benjamin Netanyahu , along with Egyptian President Abdul Fatah-al-Sisi, Jordan's king Abdullah met secretly a year ago to discuss about the Israeli-Palestinian conflict. But the peace talks failed to give an outcome.
