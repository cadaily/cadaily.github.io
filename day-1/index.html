Day 1: 11 February 2017

Posted by Anivarth Peesapati on Sat 11 February 2017 in Current Affairs

Shatabdis to get paintings by disabled artists

  • Mumbai-Ahmedabad shatabdi trains will get paintings made using the brushed by disabled artists with their feet.

  • These artists belong to India Mouth and Foot Painting Artists(IMFPA)

  • The paintings are not original and are high quality digital prints.

National Women's Parliament starts

  • National Women's Parliament , a three-day event to empower women to take part in many fields including politics kickstarted on February 10 2017.

  • Venkaiah Naidu, N Chandra Babu Naidu, Dalai Lama, Kiran Bedi and few other notable public figures presided the event.

Sudha Murthy appointed member of TTD board

  • Sudha murthy has been appointed the new member of TTD (Tirumala Tirupathi Devasthanams). Her predecessor was Sekhar Reddy.

  • She is a writer, philanthropist, member of Gates foundation

GK Reddy Memorial Award for Thapar

  • GK Reddy memorial award is awarded to journalists for their excellence in journalism.

  • GK Reddy Memorial award for 2016 goes to Kiran Thapar. Sonia Gandhi will present the award to the nominee.

Ajay Tyagi – Next SEBI chairman

  • Ajay Tyagi has been appointed as the next SEBI Chairman. He is a Senior IAS officer who is an additional secreatary in the department of Economic Affairs.

  • U K Sinha the present chairman term ends on March 1st 2017.

No news on FM – Centre asks SC

  • Centre has filed an affidavit with Supreme Court asking it not to give permission to private FM stations for airing news. Centre wants the news only to be aired by All India Radio.

  • There are 281 FM stations in 84 cities across the country.

SBI opens 1 crore accounts during demonetisation

  • State Bank of India has opened 1 crore accounts, about 1 lakh accounts on an average every day during its third quarter.

  • Profits rose 135% to Rs. 2610 Crores for third quarter.

Aditi Ashok slips to 22nd place

  • 18 year old Indian golf player has slipped to 22 nd place from 13th place after the Oates Vic Open cup .

Solar tarrifs as low as Rs. 2.97/unit

  • Solar tariff had hit a record low of Rs.2.87/unit at an auction for the Rewa Solar Power Plant in Maharastra.

  • Rewa Solar Power Plant consisting of 3 units, of which an auction of Rs. 2.979, Rs. 2.97, Rs. 2.974 were recorded for Units 1, 2, 3 respectively.

Trump upholds 'One-China' Policy

  • In a call with his Chinese counterpart President Xi Jinping, Amercian president Donald Trump had endorsed One-China Policy.

Chaste Lovers to open on eve of Valentine's day

  • Chaste lovers a 2000 years old archeological site in Rome will be opened on Feb 14 for Valentine's day, after which it will be closed for four years for restoration.
