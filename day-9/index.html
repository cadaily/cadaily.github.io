Day 9: 19 February 2017

Posted by Anivarth Peesapati on Sun 19 February 2017 in Current Affairs

Palaniswami wins confidence votes

  • Palaniswami, 13 th chief minister of Tamil Nadu had proved his confidence vote in the Tamil Nadu legislative Assembly, after a lot of violence by the opposition.

  • Out of 234 seats 122 members voted in favour of Palaniswami.

Ved Prakash Sharma dies at 62

  • Prominent Hindi novelist Ved Prakash Sharma had died at the age of 62.

  • He has written more than 170 novels.

Hike on power tariff called off

  • Rajasthan government bid to hike the power tariff was called off because of the agitations by farmers.

  • Instead the government announced the subsidy which would cost the government Rs. 500 crores.

TTD loses Rs. 8 per laddu

  • Tirumala Tirupathi Devastanams(TTD) has lost Rs. 200 crores on the sale of laddus.

  • TTD incurs a subsidy of Rs. 8 for every laddu which is sold at Rs. 25.

'Year of Apple' for J&K

  • Jammu & Kashmir chief minister Mehabooba Mufti has declared 2017 as the ' Year of Apple '.

  • Government will provide subsidy and also set up mega mandis to encourage farmers and gain high yields.

TRAI to review tariff policy

  • TRAI will review the tariff policy with regard to various regulatory principles. The review also includes the predatory pricing, promotional offers and other related terms.

Final version of Compensation bill

  • Goods and Service Tax(GST) council had cleared the final version of the compensation bill under which the states which will incur losses for the first five years will be compensated.

  • Meeting was held in Udaipur, Rajasthan.

  • The next meeting is scheduled on March 4-5 in New Delhi.

India gives a good start

  • In the India-Australia test match in which the runs in the 1st innings played by Australia was 469, Indian player Shreyas Iyer made a good progress for the team.

Sandeep Kumar creates a National Record

  • Sandeep Kumar made a National record winning the 50 KM gold in Max Bupa National race walking championship, completing it in 3 hours 55 minutes and 59.05 seconds.

Hindu Marraige Bill approved by Pak. Senate

  • Pakistan senate has passed Hindu Marraige bill which will be sent for further approval from the president.

Hafiz Saeed under ATA

  • Pakistan provisional Punjab governmetn has listed Hafiz Saeed chief of Jamat-ud-Dawa(JuD) under Pakistan's Anti Terrorism Act(ATA) .

Afganistan Pakistan borders closed

  • In the wake of deadliest bomb blasting in Pakistan, Pakistan government has closed the Torkham & Chaman border connecting Pakistan and Afghanistan.

  • Bomb blasts were claimed by Islamic State(IS)

IS business model nearing collapse

  • Self proclaimed Caliphate's Islamic state is in the state of collapsing financially because of the decline in revenue.

China to build floating nuclear reactors

  • China is planning to build floating nuclear reactors to provide electricity for its offshore projects and few islands in South China Sea

  • China's National Development and Reform commission has already approved the project.
